About me

I am a Master's student in Software Engineering at Delft University of Technology under Alberto Bacchelli. Right now, I am finalizing my Master's Thesis on language modelling of software (see also my publications), which fits squarely in my research interests of studying software properties and improving the ease of its development.

I enjoy travelling and collaborating with other groups, and will always reply to an idea no matter how odd (no really). On the side, I try to do my share by (sub-)reviewing papers, and keep myself entertained by discussing/implementing ideas ranging from the crazy to the absurd (and sometimes the interesting, corresponding blog may come).
